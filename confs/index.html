College Rugby Governance & Structure

There are two governing bodies that share control over American Division 1 college rugby: Collegiate Rugby Association of America (CRAA) and National Collegiate Rugby (NCR). Each governing body distinguishes between D1A and D1AA subdivisions, and while teams play games across governing bodies or might even belong to multiple, we only distinguish the primary championship for which each team competes.

As of 2023-2024, CRAA governs 85 teams (45 in D1A, 40 in D1AA), and NCR governs 75 teams (31 in D1A, 44 in D1AA).

Eligibility Quirks

There are teams who are members of these governing bodies or conferences who aren't listed on the respective governing body / conference pages. This is because they aren't in our system as a Division 1 team. Conversely, the D1A and D1AA should be accurate (since by definition, those teams are in Division 1).

A number of conferences separate their teams into divisions (based on geography, D1A/D1AA, etc.). We don't go into that level of detail.
