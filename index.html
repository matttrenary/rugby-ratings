RugbyHawk: A Bird's-Eye View of College Rugby

Rankings Disclaimer

  • Men’s Division 1: This website is focused on Division 1 men’s collegiate rugby. Games by Division 1 teams against lower-division opponents are included. Non-first side games and shortened games are not included.
  • Small Samples: Like any ranking algorithm, the more games played, and the greater variety of opponents, the more accurate the algorithm will be. 15s rugby tends to have a low number of games, often against a small pool of opponents (each team’s conference). The rankings for teams who play in conferences with very few out-of-conference games should be used with less confidence than those for teams with a wider variety of opponents.
  • Regional Bias: This website’s results date back to August 2021 and include 2 seasons of 15s games for teams in the Midwest/East, and 1.5 seasons of 7s games for teams in the West/South. More games mean more wins by the top teams mean higher ratings for those teams. Expect the top West/South 15s teams and the top Midwest/East 7s teams to rise during Spring 2023 play. This seasonal bias should be negligible after another season.

View Our Rankings

  • The Elo ratings use game scores to determine each team’s true skill level.
  • The Pairwise Rankings use the Elo as well as head to head results to output the rankings.
  • These rankings are based purely on an objective algorithm.
  • All teams that have played a Division 1 game since 2021 are included in the rankings.
