RugbyHawk

Your source for Division 1 men's college rugby rankings and results. Our rankings allow for objective team evaluation and are intended to aid playoff selection. Our results attempt to encompass all games since August 2021.

Rankings Disclaimer

  • Men’s Division 1: This website focuses on Division 1 men’s college rugby. Games are only included if they are full length (80 minutes for 15s; 14 minutes for 7s) and between two schools' first sides. Games played by Division 1 teams against lower-division opponents are still included.
  • Small Samples: The more games played, and the greater variety of opponents, the more accurate the algorithm will be. 15s rugby tends to have a low number of games, often against a small pool of opponents (each team’s conference). The rankings for teams who play in conferences with very few nonconference games should be used with less confidence than those for teams with a wider variety of opponents.
  • Regional Bias: This website’s results only date back to August 2021. Because of scheduling differences between regions, Midwest/East teams have played significantly more 15s games, and West/South teams have played significantly more 7s games. More games mean more wins by the top teams, which means higher ratings for those teams. Expect the top West/South 15s teams and the top Midwest/East 7s teams to rise during Spring 2023 play. This seasonal bias should be negligible after another season.

View Our Rankings Here

  • The Elo ratings use game scores to determine each team’s true skill level. 1500 is considered average for Division 1.
  • The Pairwise Rankings use the Elo as well as head-to-head results to output current rankings.
  • These rankings are based purely on an objective algorithm, and all teams that have played a Division 1 game since 2021 are included in the rankings.
